About the Game :


SINGLE PLAYER RULES:

  • Bounce the ball using the slider to increase points.

  • Catch the squares dropping from above for bonus points.

  • Don't let the ball touch the bottom, or you will lose lives.

  • Collect as many points as you can in 60 seconds without running out of all 10 lives!
